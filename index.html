About Us

HIGH COURT ENFORCEMENT IS CHANGING

Just is a digital marketplace offering judgment creditors access to the best available High Court Enforcement companies throughout England and Wales. We provide the most comprehensive, intelligent and compliant service ever seen within the High Court Enforcement industry. We drive the strategy through data, analytics and the resulting insight, manage the processes and vigorously monitor compliance, ensuring that money owed has the absolute best chance of recovery, and we do this for free. Our panel members pay for our service, not the creditor.

Our Services

ACCESSIBLE & SAFE

We have based our model on the very successful DMI model used by Cabinet Office for the collection of £22 billion Our own Ministry of Justice Authorised High Court Enforcement Officer instructs our panel of providers and verses their compliance We have an approved panel of enforcement agencies that cover every square inch of England and Wales promising a consistent and compliant service through a ‘one throat to choke’ marketplace We act within 24 hours of instruction and guarantee a 50-day turnaround for successful resolution We transfer the CCJ to the High Court for free. The creditor only pays the third party court fee

DATA, ANALYTICS AND INSIGHT

We have been building data propensity and behavior models for the enforcement of Judgement debts for over 15 years and our team has previously build collection strategies for debts from the Ministry of Justice, Transport for London, Highways England the Cabinet Office and over 100 Local Government Organisations We use a single digital infrastructure across the entire business including the approved panel of enforcement agencies. All stakeholders have access to live information We use data to establish vulnerability, define the right collection strategy and speed up the entire collection process

RAISING STANDARDS

We operate a single set of ISO accredited process across our business and the approved panel of enforcement agencies We operate the industries only independent and recorded compliance process – sending the notice of enforcement by recorded post We are the only organisation to charge our fees in-line with the strict requirements of the Taking Control of Goods (Fees) Regulations 2014
